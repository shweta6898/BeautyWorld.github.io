Nail Treatment

We offer you trendy, the perfect blend of the traditional and contemporary Nail Extensions, Nail Art and Nail Spa services.Just like your body, your nails also need a little pampering and care. The expert professionals at our beauty world use sterilized manicure kits to help your nails gain a new life.

We have several nail extensions and nail art designs ranging from a half-moon, animal and flower prints, cubes and geometric designs, bottled and circular designs to abstract prints in our special nail art catalogs from which you can choose any design your heart may desire, and we shall oblige.

For the nail spa, we soak your hands and feet in lukewarm water, wash them thoroughly, apply creams and serums and conditioners to make them infallibly beautiful for a long period of time. Our exclusive nail art and nail extensions do not get discolored very easily, and you can choose from a whole new range of latest and unique designs.
